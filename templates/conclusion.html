Conclusion

It can be seen through this heat map that bedrooms and bathrooms are the top two features with the highest correlation value with the price. Meanwhile, parking and the rest of the features have fairly moderate and relatively low correlation values.

In addition, the exact day and month when the property was sold has no implication to the price of the property. On the other hand, the year when the property was sold and the location of the property that is conveyed through postal code have minor implications to the price.
